Salesforce Real Estate Listings App

I created an Android app that enables offline storage and retrieval of real estate listings using Salesforce Mobile SDK. The Salesforce SDK is very handy because it takes care of user authentication, which allowed me to concentrate on improving user experience. I did run into some challenges, however, such as implementing remote connectivity. I used SmartStore from the SDK, which allowed offline synchronization between my device's local storage and the Salesforce database.

Ultimately I was able to roll out an app that Steele Consulting's CEO could use to look up real estate listings in the field regardless of whether he had internet connection or not. The app also reduces the lengthy process of loggingin in via the Salesforce account and navigating to the listings section.
